# Q4 plate-with-hole stress concentration example

Quarter model of a finite square plate with a circular hole. Symmetry constraints are applied on x=0 and y=0, and a nominal tensile traction sigma_x=1 is applied on the far right edge.
Maximum Gauss-point von Mises stress: 2.994284e+00.
Minimum Gauss-point von Mises stress: 3.100414e-01.
Stress concentration ratio against nominal sigma_x: 2.994284e+00.
